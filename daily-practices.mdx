## Morning Rituals

Start your day with intention using Stoic morning practices that set a foundation for mindful living. These routines help cultivate mental clarity and emotional preparedness for whatever challenges may arise.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Premeditatio Malorum" icon="eye">
    Spend 5 minutes visualizing potential difficulties. This practice reduces anxiety by mentally preparing for adversity.
    
    <CodeGroup tabs="Visualization Script">
      ```javascript
function morningReflection() {
  const scenarios = [
    "Traffic delays",
    "Work challenges",
    "Personal setbacks"
  ];
  scenarios.forEach(scenario => {
    console.log(`How would I respond to: ${scenario}?`);
  });
}
      ```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Affirm Core Values" icon="star">
    Review your personal principles and commit to living them today. Focus on the four Stoic virtues: wisdom, courage, justice, and self-control.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Gratitude Practice" icon="heart">
    Write down three things you're grateful for. This shifts focus from scarcity to abundance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Evening Reflection

End your day with thoughtful review to learn from experiences and prepare for tomorrow. This practice builds self-awareness and continuous improvement.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Journaling" icon="edit">
    Maintain a daily journal to track your Stoic practice and insights. Regular writing reinforces learning and provides valuable self-reflection.
    
    <Callout kind="tip">Use prompts like: What challenged my equanimity today? How did I respond? What virtue did I practice?</Callout>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Progress Review" icon="check-circle">
    Assess your daily alignment with Stoic principles. Celebrate small victories and identify areas for growth.
    
    <Expandable title="Sample Review Questions" default-open="false">
      Did I maintain composure during stressful situations? Did I focus on what I can control? Did I show kindness to others?
    </Expandable>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Breathing Techniques

Master the art of controlled breathing to maintain calm during challenging moments. These techniques are rooted in ancient Stoic practices and supported by modern science.

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="4-7-8 Breathing" default-open="true">
    Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 7 counts, exhale for 8 counts. This technique activates the parasympathetic nervous system.
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Box Breathing" default-open="false">
    Inhale for 4, hold for 4, exhale for 4, hold for 4. Use this for quick stress relief during high-pressure situations.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
